Sitefinity Onsite Training

Are you a Sitefinity Developer? Designer or Admin? Business Analyst? Falafel is offering four days of training comprised of training for your entire web team with a Certified Sitefinity Expert.

How is this different from online training?

Onsite training is not just a lecture; it’s your opportunity to have time with one of our Sitefinity Experts face-to-face to help you with your projects and issues. The course is 50% lecture, and 50% hands-on, giving you the absolute best experience in mastering all corners of Sitefinity. You have the option of sending your entire web team and saving, or purchasing a particular portion of the training.

Why Falafel?

Falafel Software has been a Sitefinity training partner for more than a decade. Companies including NASA, Toyota, Princeton University, Mayo Clinic and more have trusted our training worldwide. Our direct, side-by-side teaching style will keep you engaged and eager to apply what you’ve learned to your projects.
