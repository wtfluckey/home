Jaroth needed to create a logistics dashboard, from which they could schedule, manage and analyze their ice cream delivery business. The solution was the StoreVoyant system designed and built by Falafel Software. StoreVoyant is a centralized hub that integrates information flowing from disparate systems, such as the Accounting/Delivery/Inventory system, mobile handsets in the fields, and the Ticketing System. In StoreVoyant, based on roles, different users can manage their portion of the business. For instance, dispatchers can use a highly customized Bing Maps dispatch screen to make decisions about which tickets to assign to which drivers, and business managers can use the dashboard screen to approve or deny new business opportunities. Falafel built the new system from scratch, and worked with Jaroth to migrate data from three legacy systems in to the new hub.

{ Telerik

Technology

  • WPF

For Telerik products, Falafel Software writes training courseware, creates training videos, and trains thousands of Telerik customers. So when Telerik wanted a desktop video trainer that would enrich the self paced training experience, they turned to Falafel Software. Falafel developed a WPF solution known as the Telerik Trainer . The Telerik Trainer has a beautiful design with rounded edges, glowing buttons, and a dark theme matching Telerik’s branding, but the Trainer is much more than a pretty video player. { Clockwork Solutions

Technology

  • Silverlight
  • WCF
  • RIA Services
  • Entity Framework
  • SQL Server
  • RadControls for Silverlight

Clockwork Solutions performs predictive modeling analysis for defense and energy clients. As one would expect, predicting the life cycle of an entire weapon systems fleet based on the simulation of logistics decisions can result in a very complex study. These studies however, can save their stakeholders a lot of money, making it imperative that they are easy to understand. Clockwork needed a custom software package that would tell the story of each of these studies in a compelling, interactive and understandable way. Enter Falafel Software. We delivered TLCM-WP, a custom Silverlight software package that combines animated data visualization with deep data and results drill down capabilities. Some highlights of the project include a suite of innovative and custom visualization components such as the Study Animator, that ties an animated map to a series of time synchronized graphs, and a the Storyboard control, that allows users to organize the information in the study. The project’s architecture was designed with extensibility in mind, allowing the story of each future Clockwork study to be told in this new visualization tool with no additional development.

{ JBT AeroTech

Technology

  • ASP.NET
  • AJAX
  • SQL Server
  • Cognos
  • RadControls for ASP.NET AJAX

At an airport, hundreds of things must happen quickly and seamlessly between touchdown and takeoff. JBT AeroTech produces much of the equipment airports rely on and often provides the experienced personnel needed to ensure things run like clockwork. To ensure operations personnel were aware of all things taking place, JBT AeroTech envisioned “iOps”; a customizable web based monitoring application that could provide real time visual feedback of multiple airport functions. JBT AeroTech hired Falafel Software to help turn the idea into a reality. Falafel delivered an extensible and configurable ASP.NET AJAX based application that made heavy use of Telerik’s RadDock component for customization. User’s could choose to monitor an entire terminal or any individual airport system including individual gates, elevators, escalators, baggage systems and HVAC systems. Designed for large flat screens, much could be seen at one, but user’s never lost sight of high priority alerts, thanks to a RadRotator that continually scrolled across the top of the screen with important information.
