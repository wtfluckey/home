Onsite Training/Coaching

Test automation is a very hard domain, even for teams who've been successful in other aspects of software delivery. Bring Falafel's experts onsite for one or more days of training and coaching face-to-face with your team.

You'll be able to dive into the practical setup, configuration, and implementation of automated testing with TestComplete. You'll work directly in your own environment with your own systems and learn where to start building your high-value, flexible automation suites.

Detail Summary

  • One or more full days
  • Travel and Expenses not included
  • Onsite sessions scheduled per availability with a minimum of four weeks' notice
  • Agenda customized to meet teams' specific needs, starting from the Single Company Training agenda

Pricing

$3,200 per day in North America. $3,500 per day overseas. Travel and Expenses additional.
