Who Are We?

Founded in 2003, Falafel Software, Inc. provides the highest quality software development, mobile solutions, consultation, and training services available. Starting initially with consulting and training, Falafel Software found itself expanding rapidly on the excellence of its engineers and the incredible sense of teamwork exhibited by everyone in the company. This common mutual respect for each other's talents has been a major asset for Falafel, causing extraordinary growth, and a level of quality that very few other IT companies can match. Employees include best-selling authors, industry speakers, technology decision makers, and former Microsoft and Borland engineers. All of Falafel engineers are Microsoft Certified Professionals, Certified Application Developers, or Most Valuable Professionals.

What Do We Need?

We are looking for highly skilled, experienced engineers who can work in a fast-paced team environment using cutting-edge technologies. We solve the difficult, even impossible problems for our customers, often times innovating solutions where none existed before. We need team members who thrive on those challenges, and who can learn and adapt quickly from day to day.

But more than just your technical skills, we want engineers who bring more than one dimension to our team. Chase tornadoes? Surf? DJ? Raise beef cattle? We love diverse, passionate, and unique individuals – that is part of what makes our team succeed, and part of who we are.

Required Qualities:

  • Experience working in a rapid-development, agile environment with ability to meet aggressive product release targets
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills
  • High attention to detail
  • Ability to successfully deliver projects in a deadline driven environment
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills

Why Work at Falafel Software?

At Falafel, we’re truly a family. We work with the best engineers in the world using the latest technologies, and we have a blast doing it. Every year we meet up for vacation to catch up. Recently we’ve been to Hawaii, Bora Bora, and Castaway Kay. You should work with us if you love the reward of solving impossible programs and having a ‘work to live’ attitude. Well, work-a-holics are welcome as well.

Office Manager, Falafel Headquarters

Location: Scotts Valley, CA

Job Description:

Falafel is looking for an Office Manager for our HQ in Scotts Valley, CA. We are a software development, training and consulting company looking for people who are passionate, dedicated, insightful, creative, hands-on, team oriented, and innovative.

As the Office Manager you’ll be sure the office is in top form at all times, and work hard to make this happen. You will bring our remote team together by planning our All-Hands Meetings, arranging for team travel, and work closely with external vendors. The ideal candidate is relentless about the details, rock-solid reliable, passionate about people, and attuned to the little things that affect them. You have a track record of excellence as an Office Manager at other fast-paced, innovative companies.

Here’s some of the functions and responsibilities of the position:

  • Distributing and shipping of mail
  • Arranging travel (flights, rental car, hotel etc.) for the team
  • Maintaining relationships and working closely with external vendors, and ordering materials from them as necessary
  • Working with customers to prepare them for their online or onsite training (be their main point of contact, distributing training materials, templates etc.)
  • Making sure our training schedule is up to date on the website and in the Falafel2GO mobile app Keeping our office running smoothly and well-stocked
  • Being our primary line of communication with the building property manager (for maintenance, key card etc.)
  • Assisting the CEO by maintaining calendar appointments, meetings, etc.
  • Answering the Falafel main phone line and directing the call accordingly
  • Making sure there is no wrong information on our websites, especially for onsite and online training offerings

Here's what we are looking for:

  • Efficient at scheduling & researching
  • Great attention to detail
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills
  • Amazing organizational skills
  • Flexibility with workload; able to multitask and juggle ongoing projects
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
  • Desire to delight others
  • Technical proficiency; familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite, GoToMeeting, Google Apps, Trello, Dropbox (or similar). Salesforce experience is a plus.
  • Deep curiosity for how things work and how they can be improved
  • Someone who found at least one grammar mistake in this job listing

If this sounds exciting to you, tell us some more about yourself, we hope to hear from you soon!

Sr. Marketing Manager

Falafel Software is looking for people who are passionate, dedicated, insightful, creative, team oriented, and innovative. This senior-level marketing manager will partner and collaborate with the Corporate Marketing, Product Marketing, and Client Development teams to plan and execute impactful lead generation and lead nurturing campaigns across the Falafel Software product line that tie to near and long term objectives. The ideal candidate is a self-starter with a deep understanding of lead generation for current clients, performance marketing, the end-to-end marketing funnel, sales pipeline and conversion targets, B2B sales, landing page optimization, analytics and testing. The Senior Marketing Manager, Demand Generation is responsible for building and managing the channels for demand generation that drive our revenue. These channels will include SEM, SEO, display, retargeting, email (outbound and nurture), and webinars. There is a significant amount of testing and optimization to be done in terms of scaling the team, infrastructure, processes and analytics.

In this role, you will be responsible for filling our pipeline with qualified and educated prospects as well as developing and executing new strategies and plans to drive market visibility, generate demand and achieve business and/or revenue goals. This includes setting campaign goals and metrics, selecting target and reach methods, developing the most compelling offers and campaigns for clients and prospects, and reporting on the program’s success. This position requires a mix of skills including a creative flair, strong organizational skills and the ability to balance multiple campaigns and projects, while thriving in a highly matrixed, global environment. They should have solid marketing DNA in terms of learning and understanding the client base, the decision making process and how lead generation should cater for an outstanding customer experience end to end. In addition, functional orchestration and communications in planning, strategy, execution, business insights, cross-team collaboration, and budget management are key.

Responsibilities:

  • Business and Strategic Planning: Create the vision, strategy, and necessary infrastructure for scaled global demand generation channels that support fast experimentation to grow marketing contribution pipeline, transform how Falafel Software engages and influences clients, and identify incremental growth opportunities.
  • Campaign Planning: Develop and execute actionable marketing campaigns that can scale across geographies and offerings, with a clear focus on quality execution and results. .) Identify and initiate new campaigns (events, email campaigns, lead nurturing programs, etc.) and new opportunities for existing campaigns based on analysis of past performance. Must be able to easily navigate between strategy and execution, and balance corporate campaigns with key initiatives in the field to meet or exceed targets.
  • Measurement: Track and report on effectiveness of marketing initiatives and campaigns, with the purpose of improving resource allocation, scalability, ROI and pipeline generation. Implement testing methodologies (A/B) to continually improve overall initiatives.
  • Partnership: Work collaboratively and proactively with partners internally (web, events, PR, content, sales enablement teams) to get timely and compelling materials to feed lead generation programs and activities.

Desired Skills and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, communication or related major, or equivalent related experience
  • Budget and program management skills, with attention to detail and analytics.
  • Minimum of 5 years of marketing experience, with a strong emphasis on demand generation, field marketing, pipeline building, lead lifecycle.
  • Experience creating and executing ROI-driven marketing campaign and lead generation programs, including but not limited to email, website, PPC, SEO, etc.
  • Data-driven; proven ability to leverage campaign results to drive strategy
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills with ability to define and communicate issues and opportunities at all levels of the organization
  • Ability to balance, prioritize and deliver excellent results under tight deadlines
  • Experience with Salesforce and marketing automation systems: Salesforce CRM, Pardot, and/or Marketo preferred
  • Experience working with multiple priorities and multiple projects at once
